Becoming a Member

If you think your center is a digital humanities center, in whole or in part, then wed be glad to have you join centerNet. We leave the definition of digital humanities up to you, but we intend to be inclusive, and we know that there will be cross-over into the social sciences, media studies, digital arts, and other related areas. This might include humanities centers with a strong interest in or focus on digital platforms. One caveata center should be larger than a single project, and it should have some history or promise of persistence. To see a list our Founding Centers from around the world, click here.
